Optimization

Tips for Best Results

Good Lighting

Brightness is key. Ensure your hand is well-lit without heavy shadows for the AI to track landmarks accurately.

Hand Position

Keep your hand roughly 1-2 feet from the camera and centered. Palm-facing gestures work best.

Clear Space

Background noise isn't just audio. A plain, non-cluttered background helps the model isolate your hand motion.

Steady Pace

The model processes frames in real-time. Moving at a consistent, steady pace provides much higher stability.

Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Do I need to give camera permissions?
Yes, for Sign → Text and Sign → Voice, your browser will ask for camera permission. This logic is handled locally in your browser and we never store your video on our servers.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. HandSignify is designed as a privacy-first tool. Sign recognition data and intermediate camera frames are transient and processed in your local session only.
What languages are supported?
Currently, we support American Sign Language (ASL). We are actively working on Indian Sign Language (ISL) support for all converter modes.
How accurate is the recognition?
Our current Random Forest model achieves over 90% accuracy in controlled lighting. Following the "Tips & Tricks" section above helps ensure the highest possible precision.
